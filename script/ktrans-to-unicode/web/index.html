KTRANS - Kuvera transliteration scheme for e-mail and chat in hindi

        Objectives:
        - can be typed on any national keyboard
        - available characters on PC and smart phones
        - indic input software not required
        - reflect hindi syntax and pronunciation at the same time
        - fewest characters per sound
        - avoid uppercase i to not confuse it with lowercase L
